Dynamite Site

“Dotfusion brings creative imagination to Life!
“Dotfusion brings creative imagination to Life!

First Canadian Place, one of down-town Toronto’s most prominent business landmarks, found itself and its website in need of rejuvenation. Their primary audience was shifting dramatically in identity. No longer was Toronto’s financial district to be defined by stiff suits of the old generation, rather, a wave of hip, young and technologically savvy youth are settling in to become the community's future.

the mission

In order to cater to First Canadian Place's brand toward young business professionals and to boost appeal for their vast array of retail stores, restaurants, events and exhibitions, our team at Dotfusion led them on a mission: overhaul the website and get in with the cool crowd.

ready, set, goal.

Relevant content is the holy grail for their new audience. Not only should the website be aesthetically smooth, modern, responsive and intuitive in design, it also had to display personalized content on an individual level.

dynamic experience

To achieve the dream, we powered the new site with AgilityCMS as the content management platform . The visuals received a slick redesign with responsive coding, as well as several new features that provided personalized, relevant experiences for each user.

The dynamic homepage acts as a live feed that curates content based on a user’s account preferences. Each First Canadian Place business, be it a retailer, sports club or restaurant, also has a ‘Favorites’ button in its directory page. Each user will see promotions and news for their favourite stores dynamically pinned to the top of the homepage. This live feed updates in real-time when content is added through AgilityCMS and also pulls new content from social media channels such as Facebook, Twitter and Instagram. For First Canadian Place fans, every visit to the website is a fresh and engaging experience.


Personalized email campaigns are the future, and for First Canadian Place, the future is now. Each user on their website have the awesome power to choose their individual preferences based on interest categories that will determine the content they receive in email newsletters. Interested in Art, Promotions and Food? Relevant content to your liking will be pulled from AgilityCMS and dropped into a customized, mobile responsive email template. The emails are then deployed through SalesForce Marketing Cloud’s platform with full tracking reports after deployment. First Canadian Place has full control over the content to be displayed for each category, and never needs to go through the hassle of manually designing each newsletter again!

Did we mention, that the emails also look beautiful on mobile?

no feature is impossible

The First Canadian Place website is a living piece of art that scales and continues to receive improvements on a continuous basis. Exploring, pushing the limits and leading the charge into the frontiers of web development, all while having a good time — what can be better than that?

effortless management

First Canadian Place now has a simple and flexible platform that gives them full control over all content on their website. Update and changes that would have required hours of tech support in the past now becomes a breeze. The modular and stackable website components can be changed, added or deleted on the spot and still look dashing on any screen. Admins have the ability to manage the delicate balance between auto-generated pages and manually curating content on every page. First Canadian Place has traveled to the future and sprinted ahead.
