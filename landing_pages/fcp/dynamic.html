First Canadian Place newsletter subscribers are now able to receive personalized content specifically curated to their interests. After we revamped their website, myfirstcanadianplace.ca users now have the ability to log in to their account and specify their personal interests in categories such as the Arts, Fashion or Promotions. Each email dynamically pulls relevant content from the website based on the recipientâ€™s account preferences and renders the final result in an elegant, mobile-responsive template which looks stylish and fitted on any screen.

How does it work?

Our team at Dotfusion developed the solution through our two platform partners, AgilityCMS and Salesforce Marketing Cloud. AgilityCMS provides a flexible and feature-rich content management platform that is scalable and customizable. The Salesforce Marketing Cloud suite provides email deployment and management through ExactTarget, an enterprise-scale email service provider.

Dotfusion designed the dynamic emails using a custom template that pulls content directly from AgilityCMS into ExactTarget. First Canadian Place manages each newsletter campaign through their website admin in AgilityCMS and can upload content to be populated on the site. There are also options to flag individual pieces of content to be included or excluded from their email campaigns. The back-end administration, combined with each subscriber's account preferences, create tailored email experiences that boost user interest and engagement.

Complete Management

We trained First Canadian Place on all aspects of ExactTarget email deployment and AgilityCMS website management so that they have the power to act independently. Armed with knowledge, First Canadian Place now fully manages all their emails content, do their own version testing and has ultimately streamlined what was once a time-consuming production and approval process.

Sync. Automation. Personalization.

Dotfusion has leveraged automation and platform synchronization technology to innovate the email development process. With accurate, segmented emails, First Canadian place is now relevant, personal and delivering more value to their fans.
